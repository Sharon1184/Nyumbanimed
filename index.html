Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a service?

You can easily book a service by navigating to our "Services" page, selecting the desired service, and filling out the brief appointment request form. Our team will then contact you to confirm the details.

What payment methods do you accept?

We primarily accept payments via M-Pesa for convenience. Other payment options may be available upon request.

Are your healthcare providers licensed and verified?

Yes, all healthcare providers on Nyumbani MedCare are thoroughly background-checked, hold valid licenses, and are highly experienced in their respective fields.
